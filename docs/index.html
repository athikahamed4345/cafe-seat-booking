What's Included

Everything a Cafe Needs

Built for both customers and cafe owners — no third-party platform, no commission fees.

📅

Instant Booking

Customers pick date, time, party size and confirm in seconds. Real-time availability check prevents double-booking.

🗂

Zone Seating

6 unique seating zones with colour-coded cards — Window, Patio, Lounge, Private Nook and more. No other platform offers this.

📧

Auto Emails

Booking confirmation, status updates, password reset and email verification sent automatically via Gmail or SendGrid.

🛠

Admin Dashboard

Gradient stat cards, live booking management, table availability toggle, status updates with customer email notifications.

🔒

Secure Accounts

JWT authentication, BCrypt password hashing, email verification, GDPR-compliant one-click account deletion.

📱

Fully Responsive

Looks great on phones, tablets and desktops. No extra apps to install — works entirely in the browser.

Simple Process

How It Works

From sign-up to a confirmed seat in under a minute.

01

Create Account

Register with your email. Auto-verified in development mode — sign in immediately.

02

Pick Date & Time

Choose when you'd like to visit and how many guests are joining you.

03

Choose Your Zone

Browse available tables filtered to your party size and preferred seating area.

04

Get Confirmed

Instant booking confirmation with a unique reference. Email sent automatically.

Seating Areas

6 Unique Zones

Every customer gets the experience they came for — each zone has its own colour in the app.

🪟

Window Side

Tables W1, W2 · 1–4 guests

Scenic views with natural light. Perfect for solo visits and couples who love a view.

🛋

Cosy Corner

Tables CC1, CC2 · 1–2 guests

Quiet, intimate setting. Ideal for focused work or a private catch-up.

🌿

Outdoor Patio

Tables OP1, OP2 · 2–6 guests

Fresh open-air seating. Wheelchair accessible. Great for casual groups.

🔒

Private Nook

Tables PN1, PN2 · 2–6 guests

Semi-private booth. Perfect for meetings and confidential conversations.

☕

Main Floor

Tables MF1, MF2 · 1–4 guests

Central, lively atmosphere. The heart of the cafe — accessible seating available.

🎵

Lounge

Tables L1, L2 · 2–8 guests

Relaxed low-seating. Perfect for groups, longer stays, and laid-back evenings.

Built With

Technology Stack

Modern, production-ready, and cloud deployable from day one.

Backend

Java 21 + Spring Boot 3.2

REST API with Spring Security, Spring Data JPA, and BCrypt password hashing.

Authentication

JWT + Email Verification

Stateless JWT tokens, email verification flow, password reset with 1-hour expiry.

Database

H2 / PostgreSQL

H2 for local development, PostgreSQL-ready for Render or Railway cloud deployment.

Frontend

Vanilla HTML / CSS / JS

No frameworks needed. Fetch API, localStorage JWT, responsive design built in.

Email

JavaMailSender

Auto-disabled locally. Enable with Gmail or SendGrid by setting 3 environment variables.

Testing

JUnit 5 + Mockito

15 unit tests covering auth service (9 tests) and booking service (6 tests).
